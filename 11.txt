On a standard mobile landscape screen (e.g. height 390px), the absolute top bar (40px) and bottom action bar (100px) consume a massive 140px (or 36%) of the entire viewport vertical height.

Because no dynamic safe zones are reserved, the 3D scene is projected across the full 100% height, forcing units in the front and back rows of the 3x3 diagonal grid to slide directly under these heavy UI overlays.
