For battle turn actions (like submitAction or startBattle), retries are safe from duplicating loot rewards but will throw transition exceptions.
For mutating inventory actions (like summon, openBox, or enhanceEquipment), blind retries are strictly unsafe and would cause severe item duplication/double-spends.
